## Short Description

SuiFlow is object-capability settlement for autonomous agents on Sui: funded WorkOrders, owned AgentPolicies, recursive delegation, generation revocation, execution-bound metered payments, privacy budgets, Origin Critic Firewall quarantine, underwriting, denial receipts, bonded validation, Walrus availability anchors, Seal-style predicates, and final receipts.

## Vision

AI agents need bounded authority, revocation, execution-bound settlement, privacy leakage limits, evidence access controls, disputes, insurance, and receipts. SuiFlow turns that into Sui objects: shared WorkOrders hold escrow/evidence/state, owned AgentPolicies grant least authority, and live Testnet transactions show registry, privacy, origin-risk, metered release, delivery, and final receipt flows.

## What Was Built During The Hackathon

Move package, tests, frontend PTB builders, docs, and Testnet deployment were built for the hackathon. The implemented scope includes recursive Work Graphs, GenerationRegistry revocation, metered and execution-bound settlement, SPILLGuard privacy budgeting, Origin Critic Firewall, UnderwritingVault, ExposureAggregator, denial/privacy/critic receipts, sealed-bid child auctions, validator/co-sign bonds, delivery-confirmation release, Walrus/Seal-compatible surfaces, and 36 passing Move tests.
